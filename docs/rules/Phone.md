# Phone

- `v::phone()`

Validates a valid 7, 10, 11 digit phone number (North America, Europe and most
Asian and Middle East countries), supporting country and area codes (in dot,
space or dashed notations) such as:

    (555)555-5555
    555 555 5555
    +5(555)555.5555
    33(1)22 22 22 22
    +33(1)22 22 22 22
    +33(020)7777 7777
    03-6106666
